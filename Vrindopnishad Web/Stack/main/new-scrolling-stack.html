Featured Poems

Whispers of Dawn

— Emily Verse

In the quiet of morning light,
Where shadows dance with golden bright,
The world awakens, soft and new,
With promises in morning dew.

Ocean's Memory

— Marcus Wave

The waves remember every shore,
Every heart that came before,
In rhythmic dance, they tell their tale,
Of love and loss beyond the pale.

Midnight Dreams

— Luna Sky

When moonlight paints the sleeping earth,
And stars give dreams their gentle birth,
The night becomes a canvas wide,
Where wishes and the world collide.

Autumn's Song

— Oliver Reed

Leaves like memories fall and fade,
In golden light and amber shade,
Each whisper tells of summer's end,
And promises that autumn sends.

City Lights

— Sarah Urban

A million stories in the night,
Each window holds a different light,
The city breathes, alive and vast,
A present built upon the past.

Mountain Silence

— James Peak

Where eagles soar and silence reigns,
Above the world, beyond its pains,
The mountains stand in ancient grace,
Time's monument in endless space.
