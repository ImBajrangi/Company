About Brij Region

The Sacred Land of Lord Krishna

Historical Significance

Brij region, spanning across Mathura and Vrindavan, holds immense spiritual significance as the birthplace and childhood home of Lord Krishna. The 84 Kos Parikrama encompasses 84 sacred sites, each with its unique divine story and spiritual importance.

Sacred Geography

The region encompasses major pilgrimage centers including Mathura, Vrindavan, Govardhan, Barsana, Nandgaon, and Gokul. Each location is associated with specific episodes from Krishna's life, making the entire area a living testament to divine presence.
